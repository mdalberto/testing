How we protect your privacy

Psychic Source is committed to protecting your privacy and uses some of the most sophisticated security software commercially available. Any personal information you submit to us is sent through a secure channel, using SSL (Secure Sockets Layer) software with 256-bit encryption. You can verify this by viewing and/or clicking on the Symantec icon visible on the order page.

To verify that your browser is in secure mode, check that your address bar has changed color to green.  This is the most obvious way to tell whether you are browsing a truly secure site. You can also check the URL address bar at the top of your browser. This line should show the first characters as “https” when a secure connection has been established, rather than the “http” which indicates that a connection is not secure.  Finally, you can also check for a "padlock" or a "key" icon in the lower left- or right-hand corner of your browser window. To review security features, double-click on the icon.

You can delete the data stored on your hard drive in a file or folder called "cookies", at any time. Most Web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can change your browser settings to notify you so you can choose whether or not to accept them. Refusing cookies, however, will limit your ability to access some of the content and/or functionality of this site. We do not let other companies set or access their cookies in your browser.
